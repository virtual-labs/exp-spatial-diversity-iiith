SIMO and MISO

Instructions

To understand Spatial diversity, please follow the following steps:


  1. Select the diversity type: SIMO or MISO
  2. Enter the number of antennas you want for diversity and click on generate diagram. Observe the channel coefficients.
  3. Select the combining technique: EGC/MRT/SC and click on apply diversity.
  4. Observe the weights at each transmitter/reciever to see how the signal is combined.
